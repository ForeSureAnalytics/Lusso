The Grech Lusso exists in a category of its own — positioned somewhere between a private jet and a chauffeured limousine, but with the freedom and autonomy neither can offer. It's the vehicle for those who don't separate travel from living well.

Built on the legendary Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500XD platform with a 170" extended high roof, the Lusso spans 24.2 feet of meticulously engineered space. Every detail — every surface, every control — has been reconsidered by Grech Motors' team of craftsmen with over 40 years of bespoke vehicle building experience.

We carry the Lusso at La Mesa RV. And we'd prefer to show it to you in person, without pressure, without a clock running.

Interior Experience

What Awaits Inside

01

Executive Seating

Four aircraft-inspired GRECH Exclusive seats with power recline, independent leg lift, perforated leather, and integrated heating, cooling, and massage. Each with a folding metal tray table — boardroom-ready at altitude or ground level.

02

iPad Command System

Every function of the cabin — climate, privacy shades, lighting mood, entertainment — is orchestrated from a single intuitive iPad control panel. Effortless command without complexity.

03

Private Rear Lounge

Dual power sofas at the rear offer additional seating for up to 10 passengers total, and convert into a generously sized memory foam sleeping surface — seamlessly, without reconfiguration effort.

04

Mercedes-Benz Foundation

The Sprinter 3500XD chassis brings a 208HP high-output diesel engine, 9G-Tronic transmission, and Mercedes' full active safety suite — lane-keeping assist, active brake assist, and distance assist.

05

Air Suspension Ride

Adaptive air suspension absorbs the road before you feel it. The silence inside the Lusso at highway speed is remarkable — designed so conversation, focus, or rest is never interrupted by the journey itself.

06

Self-Sufficient Cabin

An onboard kitchenette, private bathroom, and silent lithium-powered systems mean the Lusso doesn't require stops, facilities, or itinerary compromises. It's entirely self-contained.
